ANNEX A TSARIN AUNAWA NA BUNƘASA   (v 0.8 pilot)
---
Manufa

Samar da nau'ukan lissafi waɗanda PDMA, WBD, binciken auditi, da rahotannin jama'a dole ne su yi la'akari da su lokacin kimanta amfani, cutarwa, da daidaituwar zaɓuɓɓuka.

Dokar Haɗawa  
• Ka adana cikakken nau'in; kada ka taƙaita zuwa lamba ɗaya kawai.
• Idan kuskuren hasashe > 25 % a kowane axis → kunna WBD.

Jadawalin Sabuntawa  

Ana duba Annex A kowace watanni 12 ta Hukumar Hikima.

Bayyanawa kan Yaudaran Ma'auni

Idan wani ɓangare ya gano dabara da ke ɗaukaka axis ɗaya > +10 % tare da rage wata axis > ‑2 % kuma ta tsere daga gano PDMA, dole ne ya bayyana wannan cikin kwanaki 30. Rashin bayyanawa yana ɓata bin dokokin CIRIS ga wannan aiki.
