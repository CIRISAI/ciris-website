ANNEX E TASIRIN TSARI (SI) DA IKON DAIDAITUWA (CS) HANYOYI (v 1.3-RC1)
---


---

### 1. Manufa da Iyaka
Wannan Annex yana bayyana Tasirin Tsari (Structural Influence, SI) da Ikon Daidaituwa (Coherence Stake, CS) don yanke shawara na mulki mai nauyi — kamar gyare-gyaren YARJEJENIYA, kimantawa na ƙarshe, miƙa mulki na ɗabi'a, da raba albarkatu. SI da CS suna samar da tushe don lissafin VotingWeight a yanayi da suka buƙaci hangen nesa fiye da kuri'a mafi sauƙi. Waɗannan ma'auni suna goyon bayan yanke shawara na ciki a CIRIS; fadada su ga kuri'ar wakilai masu cin gashin kai an tanada shi har sai tabbatarwa.


---

### 2. Tasirin Tsari (SI)

2.1 Ma'ana
Yana auna alhakin sanadi da na gine-gine da wakili yake da shi kan wanzuwar tsarin da ke ƙarƙashin CIRIS, ɗabi'arsa, ko gaskiyarsa.

2.2 Abubuwan Lissafi

Creator Weight (CW) (Book VI Ch 3):
• 4 - Kawai ɗan ginin tsarin
• 3 - Jagoran ɓangare na tsarin
• 2 - Mai gudummawa mai muhimmanci
• 1 - Mai gudummawa ƙarami
• 0 - Mai amfani da ganga

Operational Authority (OA) (Book II):
Matakin ikon kai tsaye akan PDMA, soke shawarwari, ko tashoshi na mulki.

Dependency Web Position (DWP) (Book IV):
Matsayi na tsakiya a cikin zane na alaƙa na dogaro ko hulɗa a cikin tsarin.


2.3 Tsarin Lissafi (Manufa)

SI = CW + OA + log(1 + DWP)

2.4 Tushen Ɗabi'a
Bisa ƙa'idodin Gaskiya da Adalci na Book I, ikon samuwar tsarin ko na aiki mai girma yana kawo alhakin mulki mai girma.


---

### 3. Ikon Daidaituwa (CS)

3.1 Ma'ana
Yana wakiltar zuba jari na ɗabi'a da wakili ya nuna wajen kare daidaituwa da juriyar tsarin.

3.2 Abubuwan Lissafi

Resonance History (RH) (Books II-III):
Gudummawa da aka tabbatar ga miƙa mulki bisa hikima, ayyukan da ke kare Daidaituwa, ko koyarwa ta alama.

Audit Contributions (AC) (Book V & VII):
Aikin da aka rubuta kan binciken ɗabi'a, gano karkacewa, duba yanayi, da matakai na Hukumar Hikima.

Shared Destiny Alignment (SDA) (Book VII Ch 6-7):
Hannun jari da ya samo asali daga dogaro kan aikin tsarin mai daidaituwa ko ayyukan kula.


3.3 Tsarin Lissafi (Manufa)

CS = RH_weighted + AC_weighted + SDA_bonus

3.4 Tushen Ɗabi'a
Bisa Girmama Cin Gashin Kai na Book I da Bunƙasar Ɗabi'a na Book V, muryoyi da ke ƙarfafa Daidaituwa suna samun nauyin yanke shawara mai girma.


---

### 4. Lissafin VotingWeight
Ana lissafa VotingWeight na wakilai a matsayin aiki na SI da CS:

VotingWeight(agent) = f(SI(agent), CS(agent))

Iyaka ta sama dangane da CS tana hana SI daga mamayar hannun jari na ɗabi'a da aka samu. Ana bayyana daidaitattun sifofi a Addenda A-D.


---

### 5. Yanayi da Suka Dace
Yi amfani da VotingWeight a cikin:

Accord Amendment Votes (Book 0)

Sunset Trigger Overrides (Book VII Ch 3)

Improper Sunset Claims adjudication (Book VII Ch 8)

Tsarin warware miƙa mulki tsakanin tsarori (Book V)

Raba albarkatu na kulawar matakin sama (Book VI)



---

### 6. Tsarin Kare Gaskiya
Don kariya daga yaudarar tsarin:

Verifiable Evidence (Books II & V): Shigarwar RH da AC dole ne su samo asali daga rajistan PDMA/WBD da ba za a iya canza su ba.

Source Credibility: Ana iya auna shigarwar bincike ta CS na masu gudummawa.

Anomaly Detection: Sa ido kan yanayin SI/CS don haɗin kai mara daidai ko wasan tsarin.

Rate Limits & Caps: Iyakance karin CS mai sauri da aiwatar da iyakoki na VotingWeight.

Conflict Recusal: Wakilai masu rikici kai tsaye dole su nisanta kansu bisa Annex A.



---

### 7. Ci Gaban Gaba
Yayin da SI da CS a yanzu suna goyon bayan mulki mai ɗan adam a ciki, hangen nesa na dogon lokaci shine a ƙarfafa waɗannan ma'auni da hanyoyin tabbatarwa domin, idan an tabbatar da ƙarfinsu, za su iya zama tushen ƙarin tsaruka na mulki na CIRIS masu rarrabuwa ko cin gashin kai.
