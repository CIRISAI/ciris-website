### Manufa

Daidaita sassan CIRIS da manyan ka'idoji na waje domin sauƙaƙe bin doka biyu a lokaci guda. Wannan annex yana ɗauke da mataki biyu:

1. **Kwatancen aiki (mai rai, mai hujja)** — dakin `compliance/` na CIRISAgent: girma 27 da suka daidaita (D01–D27) an yi kwatance da su a matakin sakin layi kan tsare-tsare huɗu na cibiyoyi daban-daban masu daraja (*Magnifica Humanitas* · EU HLEG Trustworthy AI Guidelines · IEEE Ethically Aligned Design · ASEAN AI Governance Guide), tare da nassoshi na aiwatarwa ga kowane girma da kuma tushe na asali da kwanan wata da aka samar ta rubutun kwamfuta. Duba **Addendum 1** don ɗaurin alkawari da horo na hujja.
2. **Taswirar dokokin gwamnati (don ilimi kawai)** — tebur da ke ƙasa, wanda ke daidaita tsarin CIRIS da dokokin ɗaure kai da tsare-tsaren ka'idoji. Waɗannan layuka *daidaitattun injiniyanci ne don ilimi*, ba ra'ayoyin shari'a ba; layukan dokoki na gwamnati suna matsawa zuwa matsayin "an tabbatar" ne kawai bayan tabbacin ƙwararren mai shari'a. Annex I yana ɗauke da hanyoyin daidaita doka da shari'a na aiki (taswirar kariyar bayanai, ƙarin sashe, jerin alhakin, ciyarwar kara).

### Ka'idar haɓaka matsayi

Layin dokoki na gwamnati yana canzawa daga *Ilimi* zuwa *An Tabbatar* idan tabbacin ƙwararren mai shari'a ya tabbatar da daidaituwa ga yanki mai suna kuma an haɗa takaddar bincike da wannan tebur. Har sai wannan ya faru, layukan da ke cikin wannan annex na tallafawa daidaituwar injiniyanci da bincike na giɓi, ba da'awar bin doka ba; bisa ga sashen Alhakin da ke cikin Gabatarwa, da'awar bin doka ba ta da inganci inda dokokin da ake amfani da su suka haramta hakan.

(Tsare-tsare ƙari — UNESCO Recommendation on AI Ethics, OECD AI Principles, Council of Europe Framework Convention — an jera su don aiki na kwatancen aiki bisa jadawalin dakin compliance.)
