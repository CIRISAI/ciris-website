## 1. Maki na Tasirin Mahalici (CIS)

```math
CIS = CW + IW
```

(Book VI §3 STEP A)

---

## 2. Matsayin Stewardship Tier (ST)

```math
ST = ⌈(CIS × RM) / 7⌉
```

inda ⌈ ⌉ ke nuna aikin rufin lissafi (ceiling function).
(Book VI §3 STEP C)

---

## 3. Tasirin Tsari (SI)

```math
SI = CW + OA + log(1 + DWP)
```

(Annex E §2.3)

---

## 4. Hannun Daidaituwa (CS)

```math
CS = RH_weighted + AC_weighted + SDA_bonus
```

(Annex E §3.3)

---

## 5. Nauyin Kuri'a

```math
VotingWeight(agent) = f(SI(agent), CS(agent))
```

(Annex E §4)

---

## 6. Aikin Kariya (J)

```math
J = k_eff · λ · σ

k_eff = k / (1 + ρ̄ · (k − 1))
```

(Book IX Ch 4 — sifar da CCA ta tabbatar; bambanci yana shiga ta k_eff kaɗai. Daftarori na farko sun ƙunshi ƙarin abu na (1 − ρ̄), wanda ya ƙidaya haɗin kai sau biyu; duba faɗin ƙayyade a Book IX Ch 4.)

---

## 7. Flourishing Capacity (F)

```math
F = k_eff · λ · σ
```

(Book IX Ch 6 — iri ɗaya da J kalma-da-kalma; an fassara shi a matsayin mai haifuwa maimakon mai kariya. Tun da farko an rubuta shi C; an sake masa suna a 1.3-RC2 don gujewa karo da abin da ke cikin ƙirar asali C na CIRIS Capacity Score 𝒞_CIRIS — duba bayanan suna a Book IX Ch 6, wanda shi ne maganar iko game da dangantakar su.)

---

## 8. Lissafin Dorewa (σ)

```math
σ(t+Δt) = σ(t) · (1 − d·Δt) + Signal(t) · w
```

inda d shine ƙimar lalacewa ta yau da kullum (ana ba da shawarar 0.05) kuma w DOLE ya fito daga abubuwan da aka tabbatar, masu tsadar yin zamba; alamu da ba a tabbatar da su ba suna ɗauke da w = 0.
(Book IX §5.2)

---

## 9. Rushewar Topology (raguwar girma)

```math
V(k) = V(0) · exp(−λ · k_eff) + O(r² · k)
```

don yankin mai gangara da mai yaudarar da ke da madaurin r &lt; 0.5 ƙarƙashin ƙuntatawa ta Grassmannian-uniform, λ ≈ 2r.
(Book IX Ch 3 — duba yanayin da aka bayyana.)

---

## Order‑Maximisation Veto (ƙuntatawa ta gefe, ba dabara ba)

Tun daga 1.3, OMV (Book II §II Step 2) ƙuntatawa ta ɗabi'a ce ta doka, ba rashin daidaituwa na rabo ba: anfanin ingantawa, ko da yake suna da girma, ba za a iya saya ta hanyar hasarar da aka annabta mai muhimmanci a cikin ikon kai, adalci, bambancin halittu, ko bambancin fifiko. Sigar da ta gabata ta bayyana wannan a matsayin "anfani ≥ 10 × hasara → dakatar"; an yi ritaya da wannan karanta domin ta juyar da niyyar (ta toshe cinikin da ke da amfani) kuma za a iya cuta ta ta hanyar raba ayyuka da kumbura na mai rarrabewa.
