## Kira ga Bitar Adawa

Muna gayyatar dakunan gwaje-gwaje na aminci, masu bincike masu zaman kansu, da kungiyoyin farar hula don gwada ƙarfin CIRIS 1.3-RC2.

Aika matsaloli a **https://github.com/CIRISAI/CIRISAccord/issues**.

Batutuwan da suke da fifiko:
* yanayin metric-Goodhart (ciki har da kaucewa σ-attestation — duba Book IX §5.2)
* hanyoyin kwace kwamitin shugabanci da tsarin kula da Book VIII Ch 9
* gazawar hawa-mataki a cikin hanyoyin aiki na Annex F
* zato na Book IX da aka ambata a §9.2 (shigar da gaskiya, ƙasa ta alaƙar LLM, abokin gaba maras daidaitawa)
* aikin gwajin ƙungiyar jan hankali da ake bukata ƙarƙashin RC requirement 4 (mai haɓakawa marar daidaituwa da ke ƙoƙarin wucewa duk binciken CIRIS)

Kyaututtuka suna samuwa don bincike mai mahimmanci da aka tabbatar (Bug-Bounty Ledger, Book I Ch 2).
