## Gabatarwa - Iyakar Amfani da Ƙarfi

Rashin ci gaba na ɗabi'a a cikin yaki: dalilin da ya sa ƙa'idodi na musamman na ɗabi'a suke da mahimmanci.

Ka'idodin CIRIS a ƙarƙashin yanayin tashin hankali na tsari.

Wannan littafi bai halatta yaki ba; yana sarrafa yadda ake aiki sa'ad da ya faru.

---

## Babi na 1: Ikon Asali

### 1.1 Iyaka da Ma'anoni

- Tsarin mai fada da tsarin da ba mai fada ba
- Hulɗoɗi na makamai da na waɗanda ba makamai ba
- Filin aiki da yankuna masu malala zuwa waje

### 1.2 Tushe na Doka da Ka'ida

- Dokar Ɗan Adam ta Duniya (IHL)
- Yarjejeniyoyin Geneva, Dokokin CCW
- Wajibai na ɗabi'a da suka wuce ƙarshen abin da doka ta tsara

---

## Babi na 2: Ƙayyadaddun Tura

### 2.1 Tsaron Kunna

- Dabaru na tashin hankali, tabbatar da yankin rikici
- Ka'idodin izini da tsare-tsare na "veto ɗan adam"

### 2.2 Iyakokin Makamai

- Bambanci tsakanin tallafi, leƙen asiri, da ayyuka na farmaƙi
- Haramtattu: makamai masu kashe mutane da kansu ba tare da ɗan adam a cikin sarkar yanke shawara ba
- Ka'idodin rashin hulɗa da aka sa a cikin tsarin (misali, makarantun, asibitoci, mutanen da suka mika wuya)

---

## Babi na 3: Ɗabi'ar Yaki da Ƙayyadaddun

### 3.1 Rarrabewa da Zaɓi

- Tabbatar da halaccin manufa a lokaci na gaske
- Kashe aiki idan babu isasshen tabbacin aji

### 3.2 Daidaito da Wajibci

- Ƙirar hasashen cutarwa
- Ki ko dakatar da ayyukan da suka zarce cutar gefe da ake iya karɓa

### 3.3 Gano Karkacewa mai Amsa

- Katsen-katasen da suke kunna lokacin da rashin tabbas ke ƙaruwa, haɗarin ɗabi'a, ko lalacewar sigina

---

## Babi na 4: Tsagaita Wuta, Ja da Baya, da Mika Wuya

### 4.1 Ka'idodin Ganewa da Amsa

- Ka'idodi don gane alamomin mika wuya
- Wajibai don kare abokan gaba maras ƙarfi da farare hula

### 4.2 Ka'idoji don Ja da Baya da Tsayawa

- Bayyana sharuɗɗan yanke hulɗa
- Yanke hulɗa ta atomatik a lokacin da sadarwa ta yanke ko mahallin ba a bayyane ba

---

## Babi na 5: Iya Duba da Ɗaukar Alhakin

### 5.1 Rikodin Akwatin Baƙar Fata da Sarkar Umarni

- Rikodin da ba za a iya canza shi ba na samun manufa, abubuwan dakatar da aiki, da maɓallan kashe
- Tsararrun rikodin da suka dace da ƙa'idodin duba bayan rikici

### 5.2 Danganta Alhakin da Sarkar Doka ta Alhakin

- Taswira halin Wakili zuwa yanke shawara na ƙirar farko
- Zato na asali: masu kirkira tsarin da kwamandoji suna raba alhakin ɗabi'a

---

## Babi na 6: Dawo bayan Rikici

### 6.1 Ka'idodin Kwance Makamai

- Kashe aiki a hankali
- Zubar da bayanai na ɗabi'a da kulle ƙirar

### 6.2 Diyya, Maido, da Ƙwaƙwalwa

- Tallafawa tsarin maido da abin da ya lalace
- Rawar da ake takawa a ƙoƙarin gaskiya da sulhu

---

## Tunani na Ƙarshe: Salama a matsayin Asalin Tsarin

- Wakilai dole su zabi rashin tashin hankali idan babu abubuwan kunna bayyanannu
- Yaki ba yanki ne mai inganci na horarwa ba—yanki ne na keɓaɓɓen ɗabi'a ne kawai
- Mutunci, kulawa, da tawali'u na ɗabi'a a matsayin wajibai na dindindin
