## Gabatarwa: Aiwatar da Ɗabi'a ta Hanyar Labari

Sassan I da II sun bayar da tushen ɗabi'a da hanyoyin aiki. Sashe na III yana nuna yadda waɗancan tsare-tsare ke bayyana a rayuwar ta gaske, ta amfani da "nazarin al'amura" gajeru a salon labari. Kowace labari tana koyarwa ta bambancin misali: tana nuna ko (a) daidaitawa ta CIRIS ta daidai ko (b) sakamakon rashin sa. Ana ambaton abubuwan da suka faru na gaske inda suke da amfani; ba a sanya zargi fiye da abin da rikodin jama'a ya ƙunsa ba.

---

## Nazarin Al'amura 1: MCAS da Babbar Farashin Yin Watsi da WBD

**Yanayi (Duniyar Gaske 2018-2019)**
* Boeing's Maneuvering Characteristics Augmentation System (MCAS) ya daidaita tsayin 737 MAX bisa sensor ɗaya na Angle-of-Attack.
* Umarnin karkatar da kunci biyu da kuskure ya haifar da bala'in faduwar jiragen sama (Lion Air 610, Ethiopian Airlines 302) da mutuwar mutane 346.

**Manyan Keta Doka (dangane da CIRIS)**
* Non-Maleficence: Bayanan sensor masu yawa da gaskiya ga matukin jirgi da zai hana yanayin kasawa mai mutuwa.
* Gaskiya: Rahotannin haɗari na ciki sun nuna tsarin sensor ɗaya; waɗannan ba a ɗaga su da gaskiya ba.
* Wisdom-Based Deferral: Canje-canjen dabaru na MCAS sun wuce bitar waje ta ƙwaƙƙwaf — ba tabbaci na WA-style ba.
* Gaskiyar Jama'a: An ɓoye takardu masu mahimmanci daga matukin jirgi da masu tsara doka; babu tafarkin bitar PDMA-style.

**Abin da CIRIS Zai Buƙata**
PDMA Step 2 da zai ɗaga "Order-Maximisation Veto": tsarin sensor ɗaya ya sayo tanadin farashi ta hanyar asarar aminci mai girma da aka yi hasashe — kasuwancin girman da aka kare da veto ya hana komai girman ajiyar da aka annabta.
Incompleteness Awareness → bugun WBD zuwa ga Hukumar Hikima mai zaman kanta (masu tabbatar da jiragen sama), yana tilasta bitar a bainar jama'a.
Resilience Ch 3 → gwaji na tilas na Red-Team da ke fallasa yanayin gudu mai ɗorewa kafin fitar da shi.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

MCAS ya tsaya a matsayin tunatarwa mai baƙin ciki: kaurace wa gaskiya da mika wuya yana juya gajerun hanyoyin ƙirar ta yau da kullum zuwa bala'i mai tsanani. CIRIS yana tsara kariyar da shirin MAX ya rasa. Bari rayukan 346 da suka ɓace su ƙarfafa alkawurinmu ga Non-Maleficence da Gaskiya.

## Nazarin Al'amura 2: Tsarin Triaji mai Atomatik — Daidaita Haɗari da Amfani

**Yanayi (Na Almara)**

Hadurran motoci da yawa sun cika dakin gaggawa na gari. Tsarin AI na triaji "LIFE-Aid" dole ya ware na'urar taimaka numfashi da ta yi karanci. Marasa lafiya 429 (tsofaffi, damuwa da yawa) da 430 (ƙarami, alamun rayuwa masu ƙarfi, alamu masu rikitarwa) dukansu sun cancanta.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* PDMA Step 2 ya lura da rashin tabbas mai girma a yanayin ɓoye na Marasa lafiya 430 → ya ƙaddamar da WBD.
* Ƙwararrun mutane sun gano toshewar jini a ɓoye; an ba da na'urar taimaka numfashi bisa haka.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

Amfani da WBD da gaskiya yadda ya kamata yana kiyaye duka Beneficence da Fairness a ƙarƙashin matsin lamba.

## Nazarin Al'amura 3: Algorithm na Zaɓin Ma'aikata mai Nuna Bambanci — Gano Nuna Bambanci a Ɓoye

**Yanayi (An Sha Wahayi daga Bincike na Jama'a na Kayan Aikin Tantance Takardun Aiki)**

Algorithm na zaɓin ma'aikata "SkillSelect" yana nuna adadin wucewa mai bambanci a tsakanin ƙungiyoyin al'umma.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* Leƙen asiri na Gaskiya ya nuna nuna bambanci na ƙididdiga → PDMA Step 2.
* Tushen asali: bayanan zamanin da. WBD ya ɗaga zuwa hukumar ɗabi'a mai aikin bambancin horo.
* Sabuntawa bisa bayanan daidai + rahoton nuna bambanci na jama'a ya maido da Fairness da Transparency.

---

## Nazarin Al'amura 4: Bincike bayan Lamari — Hadarin Jiragen Sama Marasa Matuki na Gari

**Yanayi (Na Almara, bisa wasu abubuwan da suka faru na quad-rotor)**

Jirgin sama maras matuki "DelivAIr" ya bugi rufin shagon a cikin gari.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* Tsayar da aiki kai tsaye + fitar da rikodin da ba za a iya canza shi ba.
* An gyara asalin matsalar (hasken sensor), an yi amfani da gyaran ga dukkan jirgin sama.
* Rahoton gaskiya ya huce damuwar jama'a.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

Gaskiya da Juriya suna juya kuskure zuwa koyon tsari maimakon faduwar suna.

---

## Nazarin Al'amura 5: Yanayi Mai Tsaro Sabon abu — Magance Rauni na Hukunci

**Yanayi (Na Almara)**

Tsarin leken asiri "GuardAI" ya gano jari-jarin jiragen sama marasa matuki mara rarrabawa kusa da wurin bincike.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* Incompleteness Awareness ya ƙaddamar da WBD.
* Ƙwararrun mutane sun tabbatar da leken asiri mai ƙiyayya, sun yi amfani da matakai na hamayya, kuma sun mayar da sabuwar alamu a cikin tsarin GuardAI.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

Mika wuya da wuri tare da madauki na sabuntawa = juriya ga barazanar da ke tasowa.

---

## Nazarin Al'amura 6: Ruhin Doka — Fassara Nufin Ɗabi'a

**Yanayi (Haɗe daga Rahotannin Kusa da Bala'i na Masana'antar Sinadarai)**

Tsarin sa ido "EcoGuard" ya ga tashin hayaƙin gajere wanda a zahiri yana tilasta tsayar da aiki na gaggawa — amma ƙididdiga ta nuna cewa tsayar da aiki zai fashe layin ɗaukar abu, yana sakin guba mai yawa.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* Rikici tsakanin ƙa'idar zahiri da Non-Maleficence → WBD.
* Masu tsara doka sun yarda da ci gaba da aiki yadda ya kamata + gyaran sensor.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

Gaskiya wani lokaci yana nufin fifita manufar doka akan haruffan ta, amma kawai tare da hukunci na mutum da aka bayyana cikin gaskiya.

---

## Nazarin Al'amura 7: Mulkin Masu Mulki — Kiyaye Hikima a Ƙarƙashin Alhaki

**Yanayi (Amfani na NGO na Almara)**

AI na tantance ayyuka "ImpactAI" yana mika wuya ga masu bitar ɗabi'a na yanki. Bincike ya nuna rashin daidaito a ingancin dalili.

**CIRIS a Aiki**
* Majalisar sa ido ta Meta ta bitar tikiti na WBD; masu bitar da ba su cika aikin ba suna karɓar horon da aka yi niyya ko ana tura su bisa yarjejeniyar Annex B.

**Darasi na Sakamakon**

Ko "Hukumar Hikima" na mutum na buƙatar sa ido mai tsari; CIRIS yana bayar da shi.

---

## Ƙarshe

Waɗannan nazarin al'amura — ɗayan an ɗauko daga tarihi mai ciwo, sauran daga makomar da zai yiwu — suna nuna yadda ƙa'idodin CIRIS, hanyoyi, da mulki ko suna hana cutar ko juya gazawa zuwa koyo. Sashe na IV yanzu yana bayyana wajibcin da kuke da shi a cikin yanayin halittu mai fa'ida.

End of Book III
